## Overview

This HSE Management System helps organizations digitize and streamline their health, safety, and environmental processes. It supports risk assessment, incident reporting, permit-to-work management, audits, training tracking, and compliance monitoring — making safety management proactive rather than reactive.

**Built for:** Oil & Gas, Construction, Manufacturing, Petrochemical, and other high-risk industries.

---

## Key Features

### 🛡️ Health & Safety
- Incident/Near-Miss Reporting & Investigation
- Risk Assessment & Hazard Identification (HIRA)
- Permit to Work (PTW) System
- Safety Observations & Behavior-Based Safety
- Emergency Preparedness & Response

### 🌍 Environment
- Environmental Aspect & Impact Register
- Waste Management Tracking
- Emissions & Energy Monitoring
- Chemical & Hazardous Material Management

### 📊 Management System
- Audit & Inspection Management
- Corrective & Preventive Actions (CAPA)
- Training & Competency Matrix
- KPI & Performance Dashboard
- Document Control & Versioning

### 🔐 Additional Capabilities
- Multi-user role-based access (Admin, Safety Officer, Supervisor, Employee)
- Real-time notifications & alerts
- Offline capability (PWA support)
- Comprehensive reporting & analytics
